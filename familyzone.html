Family Zone

Get ready for thrills in the Adventure Zone! Conquer high-speed rides like the Thunderbolt Roller Coaster and experience breathtaking drops on the Sky Drop Tower. Designed for thrill-seekers, this zone promises heart-pounding excitement and unforgettable adventures for all who dare to ride.

Pirate’s Cove Adventure

Set sail on a gentle boat ride through Pirate’s Cove, where families can enjoy a scenic adventure with playful pirate-themed decorations and surprises along the way. Perfect for a relaxing and immersive experience.

Bouncing Bunnies

Kiddie Roller Coaster

Magical Forest Carousel

This carousel features fantastical forest creatures, like unicorns, fairies, and friendly woodland animals. It combines traditional carousel fun with a magical, nature-inspired theme, creating a delightful ride experience that appeals to children and adults alike.

Whimsical Wheel

The Whimsical Wheel is a giant Ferris wheel that offers stunning panoramic views of the park. Each cabin is enclosed and comfortable, making it ideal for families to relax and enjoy the scenery. The slow, steady rotation allows for a calm and enjoyable experience for everyone.
